🔐 Data Privacy

How we handle your data — plain language, no legal jargon.

🔒

Your password is hashed, not stored

We use BCrypt to hash your password before saving it. Even we cannot see your actual password. If you forget it, we can only reset it — never retrieve it. This is the correct, secure behavior.

👤

Only you see your data

Every API request is authenticated with a JWT token that expires after 24 hours. The server verifies this token on every request. It is architecturally impossible for another user to view your expenses, goals, or behavioral data.

🚫

We never ask for your bank credentials

FinAlly never requests, stores, or handles:

  • ✓ Bank account numbers
  • ✓ Debit or credit card numbers
  • ✓ UPI PINs or net banking passwords
  • ✓ OTPs of any kind

All transactions are entered manually by you.

🧠

Behavioral data is yours alone

Spending patterns, emotional spend flags, gamification records, and behavioral insights are stored exclusively for your benefit. This data is never shared, aggregated across users, or used for advertising. It powers only your personal Behavioral Bot insights.

🎓

Gender & age are for personalization only

Your gender and age are used solely to personalize the tone and content of the Behavioral Bot's messages. A 17-year-old receives different guidance than a 25-year-old. This data is never shared or used for any purpose outside your personal experience.
